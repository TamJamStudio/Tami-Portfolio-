About Me

Hi, I'm Tami! I'm a graphic designer with a passion for typography and a keen eye for detail. With a background in family and consumer sciences, I bring a unique perspective to my work, blending creativity with a deep understanding of human growth and development. I'm also a thrift queen who loves sustainable fashion and discovering unique pieces that make every outfit pop. Let's create something fabulous together!

CJ Wolfe & Friends Event Poster 1

This poster was created to promote a weekly music gig by CJ Wolfe & Friends every Saturday at Disco Market on Decatur during Pride Week. Inspired by the vibrant and festive atmosphere of Pride Week and the nearby Cajun Tomato Festival, the design features a colorful wolf character playing drums, a disco ball, and festive decorations. The poster effectively communicated the fun, lively nature of the event and drew significant attention from potential attendees.

Burning Shore Event Poster 2

This poster continued the skeleton theme established after Bicycle Day and was designed for the Burning Shore concert. It incorporates vibrant and surreal elements, including skeletons, the New Orleans cathedral, and UFOs, with bold, vintage-inspired typography. The design was adapted for Instagram posts, stories, and 4x6 printouts, effectively maintaining visual interest and promoting the weekly concert.

CJ Wolfe & Friends Event Poster 2

This poster promoted CJ Wolfe & Friends' first gig at Disco Market, carrying on the skeleton theme. It features skeleton musicians playing instruments and includes hand-drawn (digitally) sneakers that match CJ Wolfe's actual sneakers. The design, created using Procreate and Photoshop, effectively engaged the audience and added a personal touch with the detailed sneakers.

Just Don't Miss a Year Poster
